Tribute to Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla, the brilliant inventor, engineer, and futurist, has been an endless source of inspiration for me. Known for his extraordinary contributions to the development of alternating current (AC) electrical systems, Tesla revolutionized the way we harness and distribute energy.

What I Admire About Tesla

What strikes me the most about Tesla is his unrelenting curiosity and drive to innovate. Despite facing financial challenges and skepticism, he pushed boundaries and turned ideas into revolutionary technologies.

From the Tesla coil to wireless power transmission, his ideas were ahead of their time, inspiring generations of scientists and engineers to dream bigger and aim higher.
